🧠 Her Research: Psychology & Human Behavior

1. Cognitive Signals of Deception in Children

Studied how children express deception in structured tasks using annotated dialogue and LLMs for deception prediction.

2. Emotional Regulation in ASD Parenting

Analyzed how parents of children with autism regulate stress in education, using qualitative coding and grounded theory.

3. Cross-Cultural Dishonesty Markers

Created a multilingual deception vocabulary based on linguistic patterns across cultures, using GPT to explore semantic divergence.

4. Confusion Detection from Facial Microexpressions

Used real-time AU data to detect confusion in learners with XGBoost, helping improve intelligent tutoring systems.

5. Emotion Mapping in Educational Dialogues

Mapped student emotions and engagement during learning sessions to personalize AI-driven educational experiences.

🧬 His Research: Bioinformatics, Proteins, Genomics and Deep Learning

1. Motif Discovery in Secretory Proteins

Discovered conserved motifs in small proteins using Transformer attention weights, revealing novel features beyond signal peptides.

2. Single-Cell Genomic Contamination Detection

Designed CNN-based pipelines to detect contamination in contigs, visualizing key fragment patterns post-chemotherapy.

3. Protein Secretion Prediction from Mini Sequences

Built models predicting secretory potential from 20–30 AA peptides with biological priors on structure and domains.

4. Microbiome-Based Disease Prediction

Used machine learning on microbiome profiles to classify adenoma cases based on species abundance and statistical filtering.

5. K-mer Frequency Encoding for Genomic Classification

Created models combining k-mer frequency with embedded species and annotation info to classify sequencing reads.
